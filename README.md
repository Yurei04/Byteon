# 🚀 Byteon  
**Your First Hackathon, Guided Like a Story.**

Byteon is an interactive, AI-powered platform designed to help **first-time hackathon participants** navigate their journey through a **visual novel experience**, real-time **AI assistance**, and structured learning paths.

Instead of overwhelming guides, Byteon turns the hackathon process into something **engaging, intuitive, and human**.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🎮 Visual Novel Experience
- Story-driven hackathon journey  
- Decision-based progression (choose your path)  
- Simulates real hackathon scenarios (teamwork, pitching, deadlines)

### 🤖 AI-Powered Guidance
- **Gemini (Vertex AI)** for intelligent content generation  
- Personalized explanations, idea generation, and guidance  

### 💬 Real-Time Chatbot
- Powered by **Groq** for fast responses  
- Ask anything: ideas, tech stack, pitching tips, debugging help  

### 🧠 Beginner-Focused Design
- Built specifically for **first-time participants**  
- Breaks down complex concepts into digestible steps  

### 🔗 Community & Partnerships
- Designed to integrate with hackathon ecosystems  
- Collaborations with communities like Maximally & HackUnited

## 🧩 How It Works

1. **Start Your Journey**  
   Enter Byteon and begin your hackathon story.

2. **Make Decisions**  
   Choose roles, ideas, and actions that shape your path.

3. **Get AI Assistance**  
   - Generate project ideas  
   - Understand concepts  
   - Get real-time help  

4. **Simulate the Experience**  
   Learn how hackathons actually feel — before joining one.
